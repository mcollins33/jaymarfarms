About Us

We are a small, family farm that started off more as a hobby to provide our family with healthy food choices. As it has grown, we have decided that we want to provide our friends, neighbors and community with affordable healthy food choices as well. Our gardens are “We pick or you pick.” We deliver our eggs and chicken or you are more than welcome to come to our house. We believe that it is important to know where your food comes from. Fresh, from our farm to your family!

Marnie, Matt and Jay!
